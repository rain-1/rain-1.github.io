Lemma 7 Localization; Lemma 4 window
✓

Lean declarations

  • Zeta5Odd.sum_localizes
  • Zeta5Odd.sum_localizes_chat

All but an exponentially small mass of \(r_n\) (resp. \(\hat r_n\)) concentrates on an \(\varepsilon n\)-window around the critical index, so \(n\)-th roots see only that window.

Proof ▼

The \(\varepsilon n\)-window replaces de Bruijn’s \(\gamma \sqrt n\): \(n\)-th roots absorb the subexponential factors and the ratio limit only needs \(f(k/n)\in [1-\delta ,1+\delta ]\) on a window carrying all but exponentially small mass.
