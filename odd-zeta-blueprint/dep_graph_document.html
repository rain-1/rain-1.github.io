Definition 4Well-poised summands and their sums

With \(s=2q-1\), Zudilin’s rational function \(R_n\) gives the summands \(c_{n,k}=R_n(n+1+k)\) and \(\hat c_{n,k}=R_n(n+\tfrac 12+k)\), and the sums \(r_n=\sum _{k}c_{n,k}\), \(\hat r_n=\sum _{k}\hat c_{n,k}\).

Lemma 19Common denominator

If every \(\zeta (j)\), \(j\in \texttt{oddIdx}\), is rational, there is a positive integer \(a\) with \(a\, \zeta (j)\in \mathbb {Z}\) for all such \(j\).

Lemma 7Localization; Lemma 4 window

Theorem 16Integer linear form

There are integers \(A_j\) (\(j\in \texttt{oddIdx}\)) and \(A_0\) with

\[ d_n^{33}\, (7r_n-\hat r_n)=\sum _{j\in \texttt{oddIdx}}A_j\, \zeta (j)+A_0 . \]

In particular the \(\zeta (3)\)-coefficient \(a_3(8-2^3)\) vanishes: \(\zeta (3)\) is eliminated by the “\(7=2^3-1\)” choice.
